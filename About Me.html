About me

As an experienced Data Analyst and Developer, driven by problem solving I take pride in providing the best solutions possible. As a Data Analyst, my goals include improving results, making the right decisions and saving costs.

My passion for solving problems can be traced back to as early as the 2010's, where I spent significant time learning how the body works and how it responds to massage therapy and other Eastern medicines while performing personal care to a various number of patients totaling over 50,000 hours. As a massage therpist, I specialized in being able to ameliorate people lives through Eastern medicine in form massage therapy.

Then in 2018, I burned out.

After some serious self-reflection about my passions and interests all while seeing how quickly the world was changing. I committed myself to learning everything I could about machine learning, data science, and the tech industry.

It led me to another passion that I love.

Now as a freelance Data Analyst I work with organizations that want to make effective use of their data; from the first ideas around what you want to measure and how to capture data accurately, through data preparation, analysis and management, all the way to impactful presentation of insights. I can support each step of the process to enable your organization in making evidence-driven decisions or share insights with new audiences.
